Project - 3 (Telco Customer Churn)

1) Problem Statement:- Predict behavior to retain customers

2) Data Gathering :- Done

3) Feature understanding :- 3.1 customerID    : Customer ID
                            3.2 gender        : Whether the customer is a male or a female
                            3.3 SeniorCitizen : Whether the customer is a senior citizen or not (1, 0)
                            3.4 Partner       : Whether the customer has a partner or not (Yes, No)
                            3.5 Dependents    : Whether the customer has dependents or not (Yes, No)
                            3.6 tenure        : Number of months the customer has stayed with the company
                            3.7 PhoneService  : Whether the customer has a phone service or not (Yes, No)
                            3.8 MultipleLines : Whether the customer has multiple lines or not (Yes, No, No phone service)
                            3.9 InternetService : Customer’s internet service provider (DSL, Fiber optic, No)
                            3.10 OnlineSecurity : Whether the customer has online security or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.11 OnlineBackup   : Whether the customer has online backup or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.12 DeviceProtection : Whether the customer has device protection or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.13 TechSupport      : Whether the customer has tech support or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.14 StreamingTV      : Whether the customer has streaming TV or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.15 StreamingMovies  : Whether the customer has streaming movies or not (Yes, No, No internet service)
                            3.16 Contract         : The contract term of the customer (Month-to-month, One year, Two year)
                            3.17 PaperlessBilling : Whether the customer has paperless billing or not (Yes, No)
                            3.18 PaymentMethod    : The customer’s payment method (Electronic check, Mailed check, Bank transfer (automatic), Credit card (automatic))
                            3.19 MonthlyCharges   : The amount charged to the customer monthly
                            3.20 TotalCharges     : The total amount charged to the customer
                            3.21 Churn            : Whether the customer churned or not (Yes or No)
